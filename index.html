Lead Hazards in Lucas County, Ohio

Click on map markers for more information. Red caution signs mark properties that have been identified to contain a lead hazard. Yellow markers are properties that the Health Department sought to inspect but could not. Green markers display properties that previously contained lead hazards that were removed. Click the Data Table tab for a complete list of properties.

Data is presently limited to 2011 through March, 2016.
